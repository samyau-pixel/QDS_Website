# Airflow Management Solutions

Effective airflow management is critical for maintaining optimal data center
temperatures. Our solutions ensure that cool air reaches your equipment
efficiently while hot air is properly exhausted.

## Key Features

- **Blanking Panels**: Prevent hot air recirculation
- **Cable Management**: Optimize cable routing for better airflow
- **Ceiling Tiles**: Raised floor airflow control
- **Hot Aisle Containment**: Complete hot air isolation
