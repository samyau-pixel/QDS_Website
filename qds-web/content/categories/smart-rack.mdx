# Smart Rack Solutions

Smart rack solutions combine physical infrastructure with intelligent monitoring
to provide real-time visibility into your data center operations. These
integrated systems help you optimize space, power, and cooling while
improving operational efficiency.

## Key Capabilities

- **Environmental Monitoring**: Temperature, humidity, and airflow sensors
- **Power Management**: Intelligent PDUs and power distribution
- **Access Control**: Secure physical access with audit trails
- **Remote Management**: Cloud-based monitoring and control
