# Sunbird DCIM

Sunbird DCIM is a leading provider of Data Center Infrastructure Management
(DCIM) software, helping organizations optimize capacity, improve efficiency,
and reduce downtime.

## Key Offerings

- **dCPowerDC**: Power management and capacity planning
- **iMonium**: Real-time monitoring and alerting
- **DCIM Software Suite**: Comprehensive data center management

## Why Partner with Sunbird DCIM

Sunbird's DCIM solutions provide the visibility and control needed to manage
complex data center environments, optimize resource utilization, and ensure
business continuity.
