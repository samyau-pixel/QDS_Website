# KoldLok

KoldLok is an advanced cold aisle containment solution designed to maximize
cooling efficiency while maintaining easy access to critical equipment.

## Key Features

- **Optimized Airflow**: Engineered for maximum cooling efficiency
- **Flexible Configuration**: Adapts to various rack layouts
- **Integrated Cable Management**: Keeps cables organized and out of airflow paths
- **Thermal Sealing**: Prevents cold air leakage for optimal performance

## Performance Benefits

- Up to 35% reduction in cooling energy costs
- Improved temperature consistency across equipment
- Reduced hot spots and thermal stress
- Enhanced overall data center PUE
