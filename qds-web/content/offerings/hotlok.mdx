# HotLok

HotLok is a comprehensive hot aisle containment system that efficiently
manages exhaust heat and improves overall data center cooling performance.

## Key Features

- **Efficient Heat Removal**: Directs hot air to cooling units
- **Ceiling Integration**: Seamless ceiling solutions for complete containment
- **Modular Design**: Scales with your data center growth
- **Maintenance Friendly**: Easy access for equipment servicing

## Performance Benefits

- Reduced cooling load on CRAC/CRAH units
- Improved temperature uniformity
- Lower energy consumption
- Extended equipment lifespan
